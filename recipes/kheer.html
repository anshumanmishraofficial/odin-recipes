Kheer

Description

Easy kheer recipe for Indian rice pudding. It's so full of flavor and very easy to make â€” it's the best rice pudding I've ever had!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • half cup basmati rice
  • quater cup raisins
  • half teaspoon ground cardamom
  • quater cup sliced almonds, toasted
  • quater cup chopped pistachio nuts

Steps

  1. Bring coconut milk, milk, and sugar to a boil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add rice, reduce the heat to low, and simmer until mixture thickens and rice is tender, about 20 minutes.

  2. Stir in raisins, cardamom, and rose water; cook for a few more minutes. Ladle into serving bowls and garnish with almonds and pistachios.
